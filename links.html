Useful data links for the DFW region:

Sometimes, knowing where to look is hard. We've gathered a set of useful links to data from across the DFW region, and for data in the region from other providers, to help get you started.

  • City of Dallas Open Data Portal
  • City of Dallas GIS data (Specifically, see this page).
  • Denton County GIS download page.
  • City of Denton Open Data Portal
  • Tarrant County GIS Data Portal
  • Tarrant County Appraisal District Data Downloads
  • City of Fort Worth Open Data Portal
  • North Central Texas Council of Governments (NCTCOG) Regional Data Center (Many good mapping resources available here)
  • State of Texas Data Portal
  • US Federal Government Open Data Portal
  • FEMA Open Data Portal
  • US Census maps and data page
  • US Census data tools page
  • US Census Population and Housing unit estimates page (annual data plus past years)
  • US Census Cartographic Boundary Shapefile downloads (Many maps for Texas and Texas Counties: maybe start here!)
  • Direct FTP link to US Census TIGER files for 2018
  • US National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Data Catalog
  • NASA Precipitation Data downloads
  • NASA Open Data Portal
  • CivicData.io (here, the files for SouthLake, TX)

Other sites that may be relevant to your challenges on Open Data Day:

  • The Dallas 2019 Zoning Submittal schedule as well as daily updates regarding the number of zoning cases submitted for the upcoming deadline
  • An overview of the process to request a zoning change in Dallas.
  • Search for Dallas zoning cases based on a date range or other criteria
  • City of Dallas Zoning webpage
